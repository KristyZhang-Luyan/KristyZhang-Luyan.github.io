Lutolence Sound Visualization

How does it feel to be a witness to an Intimate Partner Violence? Listen to the audio demo I created and play around with the sound to allow the audience to experience the intimate partner violence survivor's feelings and consider what a difference the color position would make to a scene.

Context

Domestic violence (also known as intimate partner violence, battering, or spouse abuse) is a pattern of abusive behavior used in a relationship to gain control and power over an intimate partner. It happens to everyone across race, gender, socioeconomic status, education, and sexual orientation. Every year, nearly 10 million women and men become victims of domestic violence. Every minute, about 20 people are physically abused by an intimate partner in the United States.

Moodboard

There's a lot of artwork, including films, exhibitions, paintings, TV shows and music that's created to combat domestic violence. Through this research, I collected images related to domestic violence-related topics, and I discovered the color of the injury which also reflects the devastated Lutos. Therefore, I decided to name this project Lutos, and use its colors as its main palatte.

Composing

The demo was made by using Ableton Live.

Inspiration

Inspired by a Chinese TV show "Please leave me alone", I decided to make a music demo with audio clips taken from the original soundtrack. This demo depicts a story about a husband and his wife, which consists of the sound of a man talking, the voice of abuse and the spasmodic sobs of the wife.
